About

Kevin Johnson

Kevin Johnson is a multi-award-winning director, producer, and writer. His passion for filmmaking began growing up in Copenhagen and London, where he developed a hands-on understanding of every aspect of production, from on-set operations and photography through to post-production.

His work spans narrative films, documentaries, television pilots, and commercial projects across multiple genres, with a strong focus on authentic human stories and real-world themes.

Selected projects include Lonely Boy, The Assistant, Little Cypress Theater, and Freemasonry: Not So Black and White.

As the founder of KJ Entertainment, he directs and produces commercial, corporate, and branded content alongside his own film projects. His directing work has received more than 30 international film festival awards and honors.
